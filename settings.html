Mini Player & OBS

The mini player is available remotely at: http://localhost:6969/mini-player/

Open this URL in any browser or another PC on your network (using your local IP) to control playback. The full app UI is also accessible at http://localhost:6969/

A control-free OBS Widget is available at: http://localhost:6969/obs/

Add this URL as a Browser Source in OBS to beautifully show what you're listening to, without control buttons.
